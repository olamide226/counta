## [0.0.1] - 2026-02-03

### Added
- Initial release of Counta
- Core counting functionality with tap to increment
- Smart alerts at custom thresholds and intervals
- Session tracking and history
- Resume notifications when app is backgrounded (5-minute delay)
- Multiple theme options (Ocean, Forest, Sunset, Mono, Lavender)
- Haptic and audio feedback
- Resizable tap zone
- Undo functionality
- Cross-platform support (iOS, Android, macOS, Windows, Linux, Web)
- Dark mode support
- Persistent settings with Hive

### Features
- **Counting**: Simple tap interface for mantra/prayer counting
- **Alerts**: Configurable threshold and repeat interval alerts
- **Sessions**: Save and review counting sessions with notes
- **Notifications**: Smart resume reminders after 5 minutes of inactivity
- **Themes**: 5 beautiful color schemes in light and dark modes
- **Feedback**: Sound and vibration options for tap confirmation
- **Customization**: Adjustable tap zone size and various settings
